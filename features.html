Features of Our Bookstore App

AI-based Book Suggestions

Just like Netflix recommends movies based on your preferences, our app uses AI to suggest books based on your reading habits and genre preferences. You’ll always have a personalized list ready!

Monthly/Yearly Subscription

Choose between a monthly or yearly subscription plan that gives you access to borrow and exchange physical books. Enjoy unlimited reads with no extra cost!

Physical Book Delivery

We deliver physical books right to your doorstep. All you need to do is browse our collection, pick a book, and it’ll be delivered to you as part of your subscription.

Exclusive Book Exchange

Exchange books with fellow members through our app. Swap the books you’ve already read and receive new ones without any hassle!
